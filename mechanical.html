Mechanical Labels

A durable plastic film tape, designed for high-quality and color-coded pipe identification without the need for adhesive, and coated with a transparent lacquer. These products offer a convenient and swift way to distinguish the contents of pipes through color-coded bands. Typically used alongside self-adhesive pipeline identification tapes to ensure accurate labeling of specific services.

  • Each roll is protected by shrink-wrapping
  • features UV-resistant printing
  • resistant to scratches and solvents
  • Manufactured in compliance with BS 1710:1984 standards

Simply unroll the desired length to wrap around your pipe and secure it with double-sided fixing tape.
