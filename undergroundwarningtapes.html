Underground Warning Tapes

Underground warning tapes are created with an aluminum backing, facilitating the easy detection of underground utility installations using a non-ferrous locator. When correctly installed, these safety tapes play a crucial role in preventing potentially disastrous excavation errors when identifying utilities like electrical lines, gas pipes, and communication cables. These workplace safety tapes are capable of withstanding temperatures ranging from -60°F to 250°F. Crafted from durable laminate construction, these underground warning tapes feature permanent imprints in black ink.
