Engraved Labels

Designate various spaces, entrances, offices, and work areas using engraved signs. These signs offer the flexibility to be engraved with a wide range of font styles and logos. They feature beveled edges for a decorative border, unless a straight edge is preferred. Engraved nameplates and desk signs are cost-effective and available in multiple colors. Engraved labels can be crafted from materials like PVC, trifoliate, aluminum, stainless steel, and brass.

These signs are resistant to stains and easy to clean, offering a multitude of color and material choices. They can be mounted in several ways, including pressure-sensitive adhesive, mounting holes, framing systems, and more. You can choose signs with decorative beveled edges, borders, or straight edges. Engraving can be done on the front or in reverse, with the option for paint filling in any color.

These signs serve various purposes, including labeling electrical panel boards, disconnects, and control devices, as well as identifying mechanical fans, pumps, actuators, machinery details such as model and serial numbers, and caution and warning signs.
