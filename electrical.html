Electrical Labels

We offer safety labels specifically intended for lodging complaints, aimed at notifying employees and individuals regarding potential machinery hazards or unsafe conditions. Our top-tier hazard and safety warning labels come in various sizes and standardized layouts. These safety labels are meticulously designed to provide clear identification of potential equipment risks.

  • Each roll is protected by shrink-wrapping
  • features UV-resistant printing
  • resistant to scratches and solvents
  • Manufactured in compliance with BS 1710:1984 standards
