About Us

At Al Shula Printing, our core identity revolves around the concept of identification. We take pride in being the exclusive company in this region dedicated solely to producing labels and tags for Electro Mechanical purposes. Throughout our journey, we've been in constant pursuit of a simpler, faster, more transparent, and cost-effective method of identification, moving beyond traditional practices like stenciling or painting.
To address this need, Al Shula Printing has developed an innovative system aligned with BS 1710: 1984 & ASME A 13.1 Color codes for identifying pipelines, ducts, cable trays, engraved tags for cables, valves, equipment, and warning tapes.
Our M.E.P identification system utilizes self-adhesive vinyl sheets of UV-resistant quality, ensuring a swift and straightforward solution. These sheets come in various text heights corresponding to the pipe's size, featuring concise service abbreviations for easy comprehension by technicians who may not be familiar with color codes. For enhanced visibility, our labels are available in rolls of 25 / 50 meters, spanning widths of 2", 4", 6", 8", and 12". Additionally, we offer engraving services on PVC, aluminum, Traffloyte, and steel materials.
