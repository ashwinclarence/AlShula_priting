Fire Fighting Labels

The commercial building pipe marking guide offers comprehensive guidance on how to label the piping systems within facilities. While ANSI/ASME standards apply to pipe labeling in commercial buildings, some situations require more specific information than what these standards provide. For instance, different types of water are used in commercial buildings, and it's crucial to identify the specific type within a pipe. The commercial building pipe marking guide establishes a standard for including this additional information on pipe labels. In addition to the ANSI/ASME pipe marking standards, several state governments have adopted these recommendations for labeling pipes in water treatment facilities.
